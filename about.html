About Global Auto Center

20+ Years of Excellence in Automotive Care

Serving the New Jersey community with premium tire services, expert automotive care, and unwavering commitment to quality since 2003.

Our Story

What began as a small family-owned garage in 2003 has grown into one of New Jersey's most trusted names in automotive care.

2003 - The Beginning

GlobalTire opened its doors with a simple mission: provide honest, quality tire services to our local community. Starting with just 3 technicians and a passion for excellence.

2010 - Expansion

Growing demand led us to expand our services beyond tires. We added comprehensive automotive care, from oil changes to engine diagnostics, becoming a full-service center.

2015 - Premium Partnerships

Established partnerships with industry leaders like Goodyear, Dunlop, and Kelly, ensuring our customers access to the best tire brands in the market.

2020 - Modern Technology

Invested in state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and training, positioning GlobalTire at the forefront of automotive technology.

Today - Community Leader

Proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers annually, with a team of 15+ certified technicians and a reputation built on trust and quality.

Our Mission & Values

At GlobalTire, we're driven by core principles that guide every service we provide and every interaction we have with our customers.

Integrity First

We believe in complete transparency. No hidden fees, no unnecessary services. We treat every vehicle as if it were our own and provide honest recommendations you can trust.

Quality Guaranteed

Our technicians are qualified and have the experience to care and perform quality work. Our target is bringing the best quality parts that allows us to provide our customers warranty and ensure each work is well performed.

Customer-Centric

Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities. We listen to your concerns, explain our recommendations clearly, and work within your budget to find the best solutions.

Speed & Efficiency

We value your time. Our speedy, on-the-spot service gets you back on the road quickly without compromising on quality. Most services completed same-day.

Continuous Learning

The automotive industry evolves rapidly. We invest in ongoing training and education for our team to stay current with the latest technologies and best practices.

Community Commitment

We're proud to be part of the New Jersey community. Supporting local initiatives, providing jobs, and building lasting relationships with our neighbors.
