About GlobalTire

20+ Years of Excellence in Automotive Care

Serving the New Jersey community with premium tire services, expert automotive care, and unwavering commitment to quality since 2003.

Our Story

What began as a small family-owned garage in 2003 has grown into one of New Jersey's most trusted names in automotive care.

2003 - The Beginning

GlobalTire opened its doors with a simple mission: provide honest, quality tire services to our local community. Starting with just 3 technicians and a passion for excellence.

2010 - Expansion

Growing demand led us to expand our services beyond tires. We added comprehensive automotive care, from oil changes to engine diagnostics, becoming a full-service center.

2015 - Premium Partnerships

Established partnerships with industry leaders like Goodyear, Dunlop, and Kelly, ensuring our customers access to the best tire brands in the market.

2020 - Modern Technology

Invested in state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and training, positioning GlobalTire at the forefront of automotive technology.

Today - Community Leader

Proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers annually, with a team of 15+ certified technicians and a reputation built on trust and quality.
