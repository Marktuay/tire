Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome for most services, we recommend calling ahead to ensure we can accommodate you promptly. Appointments guarantee your preferred time slot.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), and PayPal. We also offer financing options for larger repairs.

How long do most services take?

Service times vary: oil changes take 30-45 minutes, tire installation 45-90 minutes, and brake repairs 1-2 hours. We'll provide an accurate estimate when you call.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes! All our work comes with warranties covering both parts and labor. Tire purchases include manufacturer warranties plus our installation guarantee.

Can I wait while my vehicle is serviced?

Absolutely! We have a comfortable waiting area with Wi-Fi, refreshments, and TV. Most routine services can be completed while you wait.

Do you work on all vehicle makes and models?

Yes, our certified technicians are trained to work on all makes and models, from domestic to import vehicles, cars to trucks and SUVs.
