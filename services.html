Oil Changes

The Foundation of Engine Health

Regular oil changes are the single most important maintenance service for your vehicle. Fresh oil lubricates engine components, reduces friction, prevents overheating, and removes harmful contaminants.

What's Included

  • Premium quality oil (conventional, synthetic blend, or full synthetic)
  • New oil filter installation
  • Complete fluid level check
  • Tire pressure inspection
  • Multi-point vehicle inspection
  • Oil change reminder sticker

Why It Matters

Engine oil breaks down over time, losing its ability to protect your engine. Regular oil changes extend engine life, improve fuel efficiency, and prevent costly repairs. We recommend oil changes every 3,000-5,000 miles for conventional oil, or 7,500-10,000 miles for synthetic oil.

Typical Service Time

30-45 minutes

Belts & Hoses

Small Parts, Big Impact

Belts and hoses are critical components that keep your engine running cool and power essential systems. Regular inspection and replacement prevent breakdowns and engine damage.

Belt & Hose Services

  • Serpentine belt inspection and replacement
  • Timing belt replacement
  • Radiator hose replacement
  • Heater hose replacement
  • Coolant hose inspection
  • Belt tension adjustment
  • Pulley inspection

Warning Signs

  • Squealing noise from engine
  • Visible cracks or fraying on belts
  • Coolant leaks
  • Engine overheating
  • Loss of power steering
  • Battery not charging

Important: Timing belt failure can cause severe engine damage. Follow manufacturer's replacement schedule (typically 60,000-100,000 miles).

Typical Service Time

30 minutes - 3 hours (varies by service)
